About Team Zabble

Sebastian Antorcha

Creative Director

Hi, I'm Sebastian, a sophomore at the University of Southern California’s Iovine and Young Academy, receiving a degree in Arts, Technology, and the Business of Innovation with a minor in business finance. I love challenging myself and learning new skills. I'm passionate about tackling issues surrounding sustainability, human-centered design, and human health. I love learning and talking to people, so feel free to reach out.

I believe creativity is a means to make this world a better place. A key focus of my work is human-centered design and sustainability, ensuring each project aligns with both functional and user-centric goals. My work also varies in mediums as I like experimenting and expanding my experiences.

Julius Howlett

Engineer and Product Manager

I'm an engineer and product manager with a passion for building things that bring people together—whether that's through clever code, smart design, or a seriously good party game. With a background in hardware tinkering, software development, and cross-functional product leadership, I've spent much of my career finding creative ways to bridge the technical and the playful. I'm a firm believer that great products don't just solve problems—they spark connection, curiosity, and joy.

When I'm not elbow-deep in prototyping or roadmapping, you'll find me hosting game nights, tweaking homemade board game mechanics, or losing track of time soldering something probably unnecessary but definitely fun. I love exploring the intersection of interaction, design, and technology—especially when it makes people laugh or brings them closer together.
