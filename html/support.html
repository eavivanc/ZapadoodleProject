Competitive Landscape

Zap-a-Doodle enters a playful yet competitive market filled with classic party games and new tech-enhanced formats. What sets us apart is our physical interactivity and unique mechanic — the zap — combined with simple rules and high replayability. Our primary competitors include Pictionary Air, Exploding Kittens, and What Do You Meme?, but none of them incorporate real-time physical feedback or a shock element into their gameplay.
